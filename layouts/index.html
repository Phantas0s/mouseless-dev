Have you ever thought increasing your efficiency by using less your mouse and more your keyboard?

I surely didn't!

Thankfully, good friends taught me how to create a personalized, scalable, and mouseless development environment.

I never looked back.

Not everybody has the chance to be mentored as I was. That's why I'm writing a book for you to build your own Mouseless Development Environment.

The goal? Increasing your productivity, making your job more rewarding, and your coding experience more fun!

Using only keystrokes for common editing operations is more efficient than mouse or menu-driven commands, because your hands never leave the keyboard.

The Pragmatic Programmer

What Will This Book Bring You?

A scalable development environment where you mainly use your keyboard. It's more fun, and more efficient too.

Precise explanations how to configure everything, depending on your needs. You'll be in control, deciding what's more efficient for you.

How to build a personalized installer with bash scripts, to bring all your tools and their configurations easily on any computer.

A better understanding about Linux and its shell. You'll learn as well to use customizable, timeless, and powerful tools.

Mouseless Powerful Tools

Your Mouseless Development Environment will include these tools:

  • The Mighty Arch Linux
  • The Powerful Zsh
  • The Brave i3 Windows Manager
  • The Skilled tmux
  • The Uncomparable Vim

You don't like some of them? No problem! You can replace Vim by your IDE, or Arch Linux by Ubuntu. I show you the "how" and you decide the "what"!

A mouseless environment is often centered around the Linux Shell. If you don't use a terminal often, no worry! You'll learn step by step.
