Have you ever thought to increase your efficiency
by using less your mouse and more your keyboard?

I surely didn't!

Thanksfully, good friends taught me how to create
a personalized, scalable, and mouseless development environment.

Now, I could never come back.

Not everybody has the chance to be mentored to create such development environment. That's why I would love to transmit what they taught me, and more.

The goal? Increasing your productivity and make your coding experience more rewarding, and more fun!

What Does This Book Will Bring You?

A scalable development environment where you mainly use your keyboard. It's more fun, and more efficient too.

A better understanding about Linux, its shell, and many other customizable and timeless, powerful tools.

Precise explanations how to configure everything, depending on your needs. You'll be in control, deciding what's more efficient for you.

A personalized installer, to bring all your tools and their configurations on any computer easily.

Mouseless Powerful Tools

Your Mouseless Development Environment will include these tools:

  • The Mighty Arch Linux
  • The Powerful zsh
  • The Brave i3 Windows Manager
  • The Skilled tmux
  • The Uncomparable Vim

You don't like some of them? No problem! You can replace Vim by your IDE, or Arch Linux by Ubuntu. I show you the "how", for you to decide of the "what"!

A mouseless environment is often centered around the Linux Shell. If you don't use a terminal often, no worry! You'll learn step by step.
